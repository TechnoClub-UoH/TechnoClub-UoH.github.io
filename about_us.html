About Us

'Technoclub' is for people to do all things technology. Formed under the vision of Prof. Rajaram Guruswamy, CASEST in 2022, this club is a way to give students the liberty to learn themselves. The workshop is our classroom, and we do fun projects to engage ourselves with real-life skills. How many people in the world know to use a CNC milling machine afterall? It can certainly change your life if you did.
